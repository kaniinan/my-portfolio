Who is Kaniinan?

I am a dreamer, writer, and painter, with a passion for learning and understanding the world through the dual lenses of data analytics and real-life travel. My constant curiosity always motivates me to investigate and analyze the collected data to know the reason behind human behaviors. I craft these precious memories and experiences as a piece of work, in both artistic and academic ways, believing that my truly fulfilled life needs a journey of discovery and analytical insights, to fill up my emotional and rational soul.

My passion lies in using visual media and creative writing to convey complex ideas uncovered through world exploring and data analysis. You can view my research papers and visual projects in this portfolio.
